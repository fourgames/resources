# The Game Success Formula
```gdscript
# Success = Execution * MarketFit * Appeal
success = (core * polish) * (genre * trend) * (art * social)
```
### Execution (core * polish)
- `Core` must be fun first! `Polish` is how it [feels](https://deepnight.net/games/game-feel/) to play.

### Market Fit (genre * trend)
- `Genre` sets your audience ceiling (3D has the highest reach). `Trend` means bringing proven ideas or media from `Movies`, `Roblox`, or `Fortnite` over to `Steam`.

### Appeal (art * social)
- `Art` stops the scroll. `Social` is the strongest multiplier: Local Co-op is `realistic` and Online is high-reward but significantly more `difficult`.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> These are guidelines, not absolutes. Use this formula as a compass to guide your decisions, not as rigid requirements. Making something you enjoy working on is going to give you higher life enjoyment & satisfaction.
